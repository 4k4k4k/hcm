üéì Training Experience Questions

Q1: How has your training experience been with NoBroker?
Follow-up: Can you rate it on a scale of 1-10 and explain why?
Q2: Who is your trainer and how would you describe their training style?
Follow-up: What specific qualities do you appreciate about your trainer?
Q3: How has the overall learning experience been with NoBroker till date?
Follow-up: What aspects of the learning process worked well for you?

üíº Company Culture Questions

Q4: What do you like best about the company?
Follow-up: Can you give me specific examples?
Q5: How do you feel about the work environment at NoBroker?
Follow-up: What would you change if you could?
Q6: How supported do you feel by your team and management?
Follow-up: Can you share an instance where you felt particularly supported?

üîÆ Future Outlook Questions

Q7: Where do you see yourself in the next 6 months at NoBroker?

Q9: Is there anything you'd like to see improved or changed?
Follow-up: How do you think this would benefit the team?
